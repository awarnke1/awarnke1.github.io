Dataset Information

Data Origin

This analysis uses the public use data from the Texas Higher Education Opportunity Project. It includes data from about 13,000 high school seniors surveyed in 2002. More information is provided here: https://oprdata.princeton.edu/archive/THEOP/.

Summary Statistics

  • Number of students: 13,805

  • Percent male: 47%

  • Percent white/Asian: 48%
  • Percent underrepresented minority (Black or Hispanic): 48%
  • Percent other: 4%

  • Percent metro school: 79%
  • Percent rural school: 13%
  • Percent PSU (public school unit): 8%
